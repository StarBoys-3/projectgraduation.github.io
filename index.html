WELCOME

Due to this COVID-19 Pandemic it was not possible for schools to run normally as before. So, this time to continue our studies Online Classes are being conducted. At the starting (June) I felt they were very boring as there was no teacher-student interactions for teaching topics instead, there were pre-recorded videos, after listening to them I was given some tasks to complete.

It still wasn't boring until the next month started (July) where things started to get more interesting. Every month we had new 'Kind of' activities like "Project July", "Socratic Dialouges", "A Tale of 3 Cities" and the most exciting "TIE (Theatre In Education)" where I gained more knowledge on various topics in different subjects such as - Diffrent ways a Map can be used, Mercator Projection, Researching more about country rankings in various categories, Tyreek Hill, Strandbeasts, Bermuda Triangle, Biomimicry, Tessellations, Picture Perspective, etc... in PROJECT JULY, how to make effective group discussions using 6 different types of Socratic Questions in SOCRATIC DIALOUGES and collaborating with the whole class to make an complete new movie about Parthiban's Dream in TIE. The Trailer which I, Abhishikth and Bhavin made for the movie got a VERY GOOD response from the parents and teachers making it the best Trailer in the 8th Grade.
